NITS 2025 - Friday, 6th June 2025, Tilburg (Nederlands)

The fourth NITS meeting will take place at Tilburg University (Tilburg, Nederlands) on Friday, 6th June 2025!

NITS is the Network of Interdisciplinary Translation Studies in the Netherlands and Flanders. The aim of the network is for researchers in Translation Studies and other related disciplines, such as Interpreting Studies, Machine Translation or Translation Technology, to come together once a year for a one-day conference to present research ideas, ongoing projects or teaching innovations that happen at the intersection of Translation Studies with other disciplines in this geographical area, and from this platform be able to collaborate further on projects, research and teaching.

Important Dates

Abstract submission deadline: 2nd May 2025, EOD AOE
Notification of acceptance: 9th May 2025
Registration deadline: 23rd May 2025
NITS 2025 Conference: 6th June 2025

ISMT | Tilburg University

NITS 2025 is organized by the Inclusive and Sustainable Machine Translation (ISMT) research group of the Department of Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence at Tilburg University (Tilburg, Nederlands).
